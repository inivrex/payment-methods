Payment Options

We only accept UPI payments.

What is UPI?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system developed by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). It allows users to link multiple bank accounts to a single mobile application and make seamless transactions.

How to Make UPI Payments

Choose Your UPI App

• Download and install a UPI-enabled app such as Google Pay, PhonePe, or BHIM.
• Link your bank account(s) to the app.

Generate a UPI ID (Payment ID)

• After finalizing a service through WhatsApp interaction, we will provide you with a unique Payment ID (UPI ID).
• This ID is essential for making payments.

Open the UPI App

• Launch your preferred UPI app on your smartphone.

Select “Send Money”

• Look for the option to send money within the app.

Enter the Payment ID (UPI ID)

• Input the provided Payment ID (UPI ID) in the recipient field.
• Double-check the ID to ensure accuracy.

Confirm the Payment

• Enter the payment amount.
• Confirm the transaction.
• You may need to enter your UPI PIN for security.

Payment Confirmation

• Once the payment is successful, you’ll receive a confirmation message.
• Keep the transaction details for your records.

Benefits of UPI Payments

• Instant Transactions: UPI payments are processed in real time.
• 24/7 Availability: You can make payments anytime, even outside of banking hours.
• Secure: UPI uses multi-factor authentication for enhanced security.
• No Additional Charges: Most UPI transactions are free of cost.
